Life Journey

A roadmap of my life journey so far.

  • July 2012

    I Graduated

    Completing my Engineering in Computers from M. H. Saboo Siddik College of Engineering - Mumbai, I earned my Bachelor's Degree from Mumbai University.

  • August 2013

    Hatching into the IT world

    After a long wait from campus selection, Infosys did invite me to its beautiful Mysore Campus where I began my career in Computer Networks & Security!

  • May 2017

    A new career path

    Moving on further from Infosys, I joined Persistent Systems to continue my career as a Network Consultant to onboard customers into Cloud Environment!

  • August 2019

    Another feather in my cap

    Completing my post graduation in Computing Systems & Infrastructure from Birla Institute of Technology & Science, I earned my Master's Degree from BITS Pilani!

  • October 2019

    Yet another career path

    After completing my post graduation, I moved to Calsoft Inc. where I started working as an SDET, building networking devices' plugins to model network topologies in a CyberSecurity App!

  • April 2021

    Got Married

    A beautiful celebration filled with love, laughter, and cherished moments shared with family and friends. The day was a perfect blend of joy and emotion, creating memories that will last a lifetime.

  • August 2022

    Became a father

    The moment I held my child for the first time, an overwhelming wave of love and responsibility washed over me. It was a life-changing experience, marking the beginning of a beautiful journey filled with joy, challenges, and endless wonder.
